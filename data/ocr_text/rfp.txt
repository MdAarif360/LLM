PAHW S2 — Integrated Scope of Works, Technical Specifications &
KPI / Reporting Framework

1. Scope of Work

The proposed scope covers the design, supply, installation, configuration, testing, commissioning and
training of a complete Computer-Aided Facilities Management (CAFM) System and associated Facility
Management (FM) operational framework for S2 - Facility, Industrial, and Storage (including Labor Camp)
developments at Sabah Al-Ahmad Cities under the Public Authority for Housing Welfare (PAHW).

The system must enable centralized monitoring, maintenance planning, asset tracking, and performance
reporting for all facilities under PAHW management.

2. Technical Specifications

Technical Specification Requirements

e The CAFM system shall comply with ISO 9001, NEBOSH/IOSH safety standards, and must support multi-
user concurrent access.

e All services will be measured and reported in accordance with the Key Performance Indicators and
Reporting Framework.

e Provision of CAFM software including asset management, maintenance (PPM/Reactive), helpdesk,
leasing, waste, cleaning, and security service tracking.

e Integration of Helpdesk & Ticketing System to log service requests 24/7.

e Creation of Asset Register, asset tagging and barcoding.

e Implementation of KPI monitoring and compliance reporting

e Integration with Authority and Tenant Web Portals for request submission and performance viewing.

e Support for Planned Preventive Maintenance (PPM) scheduling and execution.

e Lifecycle management module for replacement forecasting and warranty tracking.

e Reactive maintenance workflow linked with Helpdesk.

e Tenant and lease administration module and customer care dashboard.

e Development of dashboards and automated reports for PAHW and FM entity.

e Modules to record, monitor, and report security patrols, cleaning schedules, pest control, and waste
disposal performance.

e Setup of required server, database, and backup environment (cloud).

e Integration with existing BMS / EMS / CCTV (if applicable).

e Delivery of full operational documentation and digital user manuals.

e Submission of final training and acceptance report after testing and commissioning

Optional Scope (Management Requirements)

e Legal Case Recording.

e Sales - Sales Process

e Sites Management (Warehouse + Locations + Vacancy + Space Available + Pricing)
e Contract Management: Contract Lifecycle

e Invoicing and Payments:(Integration with Financial System)

e Customer Relationship Management

e Business Intelligence Dashboards and Reports

e Special Portals for Customer & Government Communication
